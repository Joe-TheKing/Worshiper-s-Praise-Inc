Our Story

Mid-2015

The Vision Begins

While on a school-related visit to Sakote, Nhyira felt a strong burden to begin a music ministry, pondering it prayerfully.

November 2015

Yielding to the Call

After much deliberation and a growing burden, Nhyira yielded to the call to establish the ministry.

November 22, 2015

First Official Meeting

The inaugural meeting of Worshipper's Praise Incorporated was held in Lecture Room 5 of the University for Development Studies' Library block with eleven pioneers. The vision and mission were explained, and prayers were offered.

Our Vision

A worldwide ministry that turns the hearts of a myriad of people towards Jesus Christ and emphasizes daily communion with His Spirit.

Our Mission

To train Christian musicians and reach out to souls through music.

Our Core Values

Holiness & Right Living

Willingness & Commitment

Discipline & Diligence

Integrity

Responsibility

Aims and Objectives

  • To train Christian musicians in the praise and worship ministry
  • To lead congregations of believers to offer worship and praise to God in spirit, in truth, and the beauty of holiness
  • To deepen and strengthen personal faith in Christ Jesus and the spiritual life of members through the study of the Bible, prayer, and fellowship
  • To inculcate into the membership, a lifestyle of modesty and decency
  • To carry out evangelism at various locations across the country
  • To help members to identify and develop their ministries and leadership potentials

Membership

Our membership is open to all interested Christians who are prepared to be committed to the vision, mission, aims, objectives, and values of the ministry, all of which are based on the principles set out in the Bible. All members must sign a commitment and membership form to signify their agreement with the group's principles and membership.

Our Chapters

Tamale

We have a vibrant group of ministers in Tamale championing the course of the ministry. This includes various professionals and students. Being the first Chapter of WPI, it produced the earliest members of the ministry and currently has the largest numbers.

Contact: tamale@wpighana.com

Wa

Having started in 2020, the Wa Chapter has grown to become a notable ministry in the town. Just like in Tamale, it comprises members from various professions who will stop at nothing to ensure that they excel in the work of God while excelling in their professional fields.

Contact: wa@wpighana.com

Accra

The Accra chapter is the youngest of the ministry. As it is located in arguably the busiest city in the country, the members are aware of the magnitude of the task to lift Christ high, amidst the hustle and bustle that so easily captures the hearts and minds of people, including those who call upon the name of the Lord.

Contact: accra@wpighana.com

Basis of Faith

We believe that the Bible is the foundational guide for Christian living; it is as light shining in darkness; it is the surest word of prophecy. We believe that all scripture is giving by the inspiration of God and is profitable for doctrine, reproof, correction, and instruction in righteousness, for the purpose of perfecting God’s people and equipping them with the necessary resources for purposeful and godly living. We also believe that God’s word is eternal and unchanging.
(II Timothy 3:16-17, II Peter1:19-21, Isaiah 40:8)

We believe in the one true triune God (One God, in three persons – Father, Son, and Spirit).
(Psalm 90:2, Isaiah 46:9, Deuteronomy 6:4, Matthew 28:19)

We believe that all men are born sinners and have fallen short of the glory of God. Men are, by nature, children of wrath and without God until they are saved by Jesus Christ.
(Romans 3:23, Ephesians 2:1-3, 12)

We believe that Jesus, the Son of God, was born in the flesh to save all mankind through his death, burial, and resurrection, and without Him, no one has access to the Father.
(John 3:16, John14:6, Isaiah 9:6)

We believe that all men need to repent from their sins, and all believer need to repent from dead works. We also believe that we are regenerated by the Holy Spirit. We are also sanctified and justified by God.
(Hebrews 6:1, Romans 8:30, Titus 3:5, Ephesians 5:26)

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Ghost, His diverse gifts which are given for the profit of all, and the fruit of the Spirit, against which there is no law.
(Acts 1:8, Galatians 5:22, I Corinthians 12:4-11)

We believe that Jesus Christ is our healer and has empowered us to heal the sick in His name, and that the prayer of faith saves the sick.
(James 5:15-16, Matthew 15:30, Acts 3:1-8)

We believe in the principle of giving according to scripture in freewill offerings and in tithing by faith, but not by law.
(Malachi 3:10, Luke 6:38, Acts 4:34-35)

We believe in the grace of God as a necessary condition for salvation and for living a glorious Christian life. We also believe that the grace of God teaches us to deny ungodliness and the world, and to live as God will have us do, in this world.
(Ephesians 2:8, Titus 2:11-12, Romans 6:14)

We believe in the return of our Lord Jesus Christ. He will return with his reward to give to everyone according to their works. We also believe in life after this world.
(I Thessalonians 4:16-17, Revelations 22:12, Revelations 20:4)
